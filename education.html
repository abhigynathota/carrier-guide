Government Scholarship

Kind Circle Scholorship

Kind Circle Scholarship for Meritorious Students is an initiative to support academically meritorious students coming from under-privileged section of society to build a foundation for their bright future.

Post-Matric Scholarship

Post Matric Scholarship scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme and it is implemented through State Governments and UT administrations.

Prime Minister’s Scholarship

The ‘Prime Minister’s Scholarship Scheme (PMSS)’ ’ is being implemented to encourage technical and post-graduate education for the widows and wards of the deceased/ex-service personnel of Armed Forces.

Non-Government Scholarship

Sitaram Jindal Scholorship

Candidates pursuing an undergraduate or postgraduate degree in engineering or medicine (except MDS) are eligible for the Sitaram Jindal foundation scholarship. Students pursuing undergraduate courses and post-graduation courses can apply for this scholarship.

K.C.Mahendra Scholarship

AKF brings together human, financial and technical resources to address some of the challenges faced by the poorest and most marginalised communities in the world.

Swami Dayanand Scholarship

The Foundation (SDEF) provides Merit-cum-Means Scholarships to exceptionally talented but poor students who cannot afford to study at reputed colleges, owing to financial constraints.
